So who am I? I am 33 years old, I live in Nacka just outside Stockholm.

Education

I am a Media Technology student at The Royal Institute of Technology in Stockholm (KTH), currently in my fourth of five years of studies. The programme is interdisciplinary, combining the latest technology with design for various different media. We focus on how human communications is supported by technology and other than learning about programming, math and physics we also take courses about games, visualization, interaction and social aspects of media. For my own masters degree, I have chosen the track Visual Media, in which there is focus on information visualization and interaction.

Professionally

Before starting my education at KTH, I worked for many years as an IT support technician and/or consultant. I began my career in this particular profession two years after graduating high school, in 2007, when I took a position at the internal IT department at Ernst & Young. During 2.5 years working there I provided first line support for over 1000 users all over Sweden, and to some extent in other countries as well. In 2010 I left Ernst & Young and instead joined Excanto as an IT consultant, which I felt was an interesting challenge and a next step for me. Most of my assignments had me visiting customers out on location, which ensured close contact with end users. I feel that this is something I take with me when learning about and implementing user centered design, for instance. I've since had some extra jobs during my time at KTH. This past summer I was an intern at Prototyp, working with web development for them. It was a very good opportunity for me as I was able to be part of an actual team and work for a real client.
